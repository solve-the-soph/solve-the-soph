I am a junior from NTHU, double majoring in Foreign Languages and Literature and also Economics.
嗨一～我是索菲我是目前就讀清大的大三學生，雙主修外文與經濟。我其實覺喬怡華，並且很以我的姓氏感到驕傲，但自從高中後大家幾乎都叫我的英文名字了。而我也喜歡，因為我是黛安娜·韋恩·瓊斯，《霍爾的移動城堡》忠實粉絲。

Just ME

I enjoy listening to indie music and going to music festivals and live concert in my free time. The music composed by local Taiwanese bands are mesmerizing, and each band has exclusive, distinct styles. I reflect a lot from their music and learn to feel the world I am living through the songs.
平常我喜歡聽獨立樂團，種曲風、各種態度的都行。他們對我有很深的影響，也讓我透過不同的角度感受及觀察生活。

I also LOVE what I am learning at NTHU a lot. Beside my majors, I have also taken several classes about anthropology, gender/sex and art. I am also learning programming:)
我非常喜愛自己在清大學習的一切。除了必修課程外，我也修過一些人類學以及性、型別相關的課程。現在我也正在學習寫程式。

C L U B

I am passionate, imaginative and love exploring. I devoted a great amount of my free time to Aboriginal Cultural Club, where members hold summer and winter camps for aboriginal kids from rural mountain areas of Hsinchu. We also stayed at local tribe for two weeks during the summer vacation. It was a memorable period of time, when I discovered and learned new things every day. We help with farm work at dawn, and play with the children in the afternoon. We also cooked for ourselves and boiled the water we needed for shower with bamboo.
我是個天馬行空又熱情的人，並熱愛探險。大學大把空閒時間，我拿來參加原住民文化社。在那裏，我們舉半冬夏另營，也去住在尖石鄉的部落整整兩個星期。在那裡每天都有新的體驗。白天幫忙農忙，下午燒竹子洗造、自己煮飯，並和小朋友玩耍。
